What's BMI?

Body Mass Index Is a Good Gauge of Body Fat.
The most basic definition of overweight and obesity is having too much body fat-so much so that it “presents a risk to health.”
A reliable way to determine whether a person has too much body fat is to calculate the ratio of their weight to their height squared.
